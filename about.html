About Us

I Am Still Fat was founded in 2018 by Taurez Hatchett in Warren, Michigan. Our brand was created with the mission of embracing body positivity and delivering comfort-focused fashion that doesn’t compromise on style.

We believe clothing should empower people to feel good about themselves, no matter their size. Our sweats collection blends premium quality with bold designs that stand out in any crowd.

Since our founding, we’ve expanded to offer a range of streetwear-inspired designs and continue to innovate with every release.
